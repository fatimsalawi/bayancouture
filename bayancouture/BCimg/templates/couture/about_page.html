About Us

BayanCouture is a feminine line online boutique that offers you unique abaya's designs with a focus on the best fabrics for all seasons and occasions. Founded in 2018 by Bayan Alawi, BayanCouture has come a long way from its beginnings. We focus on giving the customer an elegant piece with a touch of uniqueness. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of every woman's closet.

Explore our collections and experience the artistry behind BayanCouture. Join us in celebrating individuality, elegance, and the beauty of self-expression through fashion.
